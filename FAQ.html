Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Which brands do you service/specialize in?

We work on Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Aston Martin, McLaren, Lamborghini, Porsche, and other foreign vehicles. If you do not see your brand listed, please feel free to Contact Us and see if we can service your vehicle!

Are walk-in services available?

Yes! Walk-in services are available, but the wait time can vary depending on how busy the shop is. We recommend calling to check for availability before coming to the shop.

Is the shop open on weekends?

No, 9010 Motorsports is closed on the weekends. Even though walk-in services are unavailable, work is still being done on a daily basis.

If I flash/tune my ECU, will I void my warranty?

Because ECU tuning requires adjustment to the factory settings of the ECU, or completely uploading a new map into the ECU, it will potentially void its warranty.

Are performance parts available to purchase/order?

We are a PremierTuningGroup (PTG) dealer, and can order parts from the company to enhance the performance of AMG’s electronics and hardware. For brands other than Mercedes, please further inquire about your vehicle.

What if the service I am looking for is not listed?

If the service you are looking for is not listed in the services section, please Contact Us by providing your information with a brief message or question and we will get back to you within 2-5 business days.
