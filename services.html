Services

Oil Changes

Ensure your vehicle runs smoothly and efficiently with our oil change service. Regular oil changes are essential for maintaining your vehicle's engine health and performance to prevent carbon and varnish buildup which is detrimental to the engine. All makes and models can be serviced. (Lamborghini, Mercedes, McLaren, Aston Martin, BMW, etc.)

Service A

The casual service recommended by Mercedes at approximately every 10,000, 30,000, 50,000, 70,000, 90,000, miles.

For tuned/modified Mercedes, we recommend cutting that interval in half which is roughly every 5-7,000 miles.

Service B

Similar to Service A, it varies with the vehicle’s specifics and is not time dependent. You will need a Service B at approximately 20,000, 40,000, 60,000, 80,000, 100,000 miles (etc.). The exact time is determined by the vehicle’s Flexible Service System.

The service includes the following:

  • Synthetic motor oil replacement
  • Oil filter replacement
  • Cabin dust/combination filter replacement
  • Brake component inspection, Brake fluid exchange
  • Tire inflation check and correction
  • All fluid level checks and corrections (dependent on factory-recommended service intervals for your vehicle’s year and model.)
  • Reset maintenance counter

Brake Service

The most critical safety components of your vehicle which requires regular maintenance is essential to keep them functioning optimally. Our brake service includes a thorough inspection of your braking system to identify any issues or wear and tear. We also perform Brake Fluid Services which are recommended every 2 years.
